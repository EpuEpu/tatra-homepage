Our clients

Services

Embedded Software Development & Consultancy

We craft embedded software that's clear, reliable, and ready for space—every single time. From thorough code reviews and careful audits to fully documented solutions, our software is built for longevity, ease of use and maintainability.

Automated Test Setup from Scratch

We specialize in creating automated test setups and integrating them into continuous integration pipelines like GitLab CI. This enhances the reliability and efficiency of your testing processes, leading to more robust devices.

Mission Support

Our mission support services include planning in-orbit tests, integrating with ground stations, debugging issues that arise in Space, and designing comprehensive test scenarios to ensure mission success.

Payload Integration Services

We assist in integrating Commercial Off-The-Shelf (COTS) components into satellite buses and develop On-Board Computer (OBC) software, streamlining the payload integration process.
