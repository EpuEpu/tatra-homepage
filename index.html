About us

Tatraspace was founded in 2024 by Software Engineers with strong knowledge of Embedded and Cloud applications. As a Software House, we have solid experience with the New Space industry. The people on our team, before joining Tatraspace, gained experience in companies like Dolby, Intel, Nokia, and SatRev. Thanks to this we have a diverse field of expertise and can help with any challenge you may face.
