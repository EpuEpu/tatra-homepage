About us

Tatraspace is a dedicated software house specializing in space embedded systems. As a startup, we have begun making our mark in the New Space industry through collaborations with companies like Revolv Space. Our team of passionate experts brings valuable experience from the space industry, focusing strongly on code quality and testability.

We believe that automated testing and remote access should be standard practices in the embedded systems industry. Committed to ensuring that your product performs reliably every time, we aim to accelerate your product development by enhancing code quality and readability. By providing remote access to your laboratory equipment, we eliminate geographical barriers, connecting your hardware with the right engineers wherever they are.

We simplify automated testing processes to make your devices more robust, and through a continuous integration approach, we ensure that every new piece of code works seamlessly. With our growing expertise, we are ready to support your space ambitions.

Our clients

Embedded Software Development & Consultancy

We provide comprehensive code reviews and audits, developing software for embedded devices with a strong emphasis on quality. Our software comes with extensive documentation and testing, facilitating straightforward future development.

Automated Test Setup from Scratch

We specialize in creating automated test setups and integrating them into continuous integration pipelines like GitLab CI. This enhances the reliability and efficiency of your testing processes, leading to more robust devices.

Remote Access to Hardware

We set up remote access to your lab equipment, enabling embedded software development from anywhere in the world. This service overcomes geographical limitations, connecting your hardware with the perfect engineers regardless of their location.

Mission Support

Our mission support services include planning in-orbit tests, integrating with ground stations, debugging issues that arise in Space, and designing comprehensive test scenarios to ensure mission success.

Payload Integration Services

We assist in integrating Commercial Off-The-Shelf (COTS) components into satellite buses and develop On-Board Computer (OBC) software, streamlining the payload integration process.

Testing as a Service

We analyze your product to identify weak points and uncover potential pitfalls that could become critical in orbit. By helping you mitigate these risks, we enhance the reliability and success of your mission.
