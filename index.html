Services

Embedded Software Development & Consultancy

We craft embedded software that's clear, reliable, and ready for space—every single time. From thorough code reviews and careful audits to fully documented solutions, our software is built for longevity, ease of use and maintainability.

Automated Testing & Quality Assurance

Great software deserves great testing. We build automated test environments, integrate seamless CI/CD pipelines (like GitLab CI), and continuously refine your software to eliminate risks before liftoff. Let us enhance your product's resilience—giving your team confidence at every stage of development.

Mission Support

Having your device in orbit demands robust mission plans, and that's exactly what we deliver. From detailed in-orbit test planning and ground station integration to proactive debugging and test scenario design, we provide comprehensive support throughout your mission lifecycle. We're here to ensure your project reaches orbit ready for success.

Payload Integration Services

Integrating payloads shouldn't slow you down—and with us, it won't. We streamline the integration of COTS components with your satellite bus, developing robust OBC software tailored for space-grade reliability. Focus on your mission goals; we'll handle the complexity and deliver results.
